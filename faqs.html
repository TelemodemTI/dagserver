General

What is Dagserver?

Dag Server is an Open Source project that aims to provide a tool to integrate all types of data and applications in your IT project, or the business environment of your business. It is based on different concepts, libraries and opensource artifacts, which allow, by working together, to have one of the most versatile tools on the market.
